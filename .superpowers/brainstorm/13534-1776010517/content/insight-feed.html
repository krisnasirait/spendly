Insight Feed — Layout Variations

How should multiple insights be arranged on the dashboard?

A

Stacked Card Feed

Full-width cards stacked vertically. One prominent insight per card. Clean, scannable.

B

2-Column Grid

Two-column grid of smaller insight cards. Denser, shows more at once.

C

Horizontal Carousel

Swipeable row of insight cards. Saves vertical space, more app-like feel.
