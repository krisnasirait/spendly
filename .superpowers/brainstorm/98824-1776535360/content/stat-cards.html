Stat Cards on Mobile

How should the 4 stat cards display on mobile?

A

Single Column Stack

Each stat card full width, larger text. Easy to scan, lots of vertical scroll. Shows most data.

B

2x2 Grid (Recommended)

Compact but still 4 visible. Each card has larger text, more readable than 4-col. Good balance.
