Add / Edit Transaction Panel (Mobile)

How should forms open on mobile — side panel or bottom sheet?

A

Side Panel (Current)

Slides in from right, 400px wide. On desktop it covers partial screen. On mobile it may feel like a full-screen takeover anyway.

B

Bottom Sheet (Recommended)

Slides up from bottom — more natural on mobile, thumb-friendly, easy to drag down to dismiss. Takes ~80% of screen height. Better mobile feel.
